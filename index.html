About Us

This is an example social project to provide photography service at an affordable price to help motivate the workers and provide joy to the people in creating memories and crafting art work via the image & video editing service. It is meant at the moment in 1 location in Berlin and the online services like foto & video editing service could be done remote after recieving a sample ecopy of teh file. We are a young dynamic team of Media enthusiast and you can contact us or book our services via the link in the menu bar.
